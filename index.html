What is Blood?

Blood is a life-sustaining fluid that transports oxygen, nutrients, hormones, and removes waste. It also plays a key role in immunity and healing.

✓ Oxygen Transport ✓ Nutrient Delivery ✓ Immune Defense ✓ Waste Removal

Blood Components Used in Transfusion

In modern medicine, patients are rarely given whole blood. Instead, specific components are transfused based on medical needs, called Component Therapy.

🔴

Red Blood Cells (RBCs)

Function

Carry oxygen

Used In

Anemia, surgeries, trauma, blood loss

🟡

Plasma

Function

Contains proteins and clotting factors

Used In

Liver disease, burns, clotting disorders

⚪

Platelets

Function

Help in blood clotting

Used In

Dengue, cancer therapy, low platelet count

🧪

Cryoprecipitate

Function

Rich in clotting factors (fibrinogen)

Used In

Severe bleeding, hemophilia

👉 One donation can help up to 3 patients

Component therapy maximizes the impact of every single donation.

Rare Blood Types

🟡

Golden Blood (Rh-null)

  • Known as the rarest blood type in the world
  • Lacks all Rh antigens
  • Extremely rare, around 1 in 6 million people
  • Can donate to all Rh types but can only receive from Rh-null

👉 Highly valuable for medical research and rare transfusions

🔴

Bombay Blood Group (hh)

  • First discovered in Mumbai, India
  • Appears like O group but is genetically different
  • Cannot receive blood from O group
  • Can receive blood only from another Bombay group donor

👉 Extremely critical and requires specialized matching

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers for patients, donors, hospitals, and search engines looking for Bloodwaale.

What is Bloodwaale?

Bloodwaale is a blood support platform that connects donors, patients, hospitals, and blood banks across India.

How can I raise a blood request on Bloodwaale?

Open the Raise Blood Request page, submit the patient details, blood group, hospital, city, and urgency level, and the team can coordinate matching sources.

How do I register as a donor?

Visit the Donate Blood page and submit your name, age, contact details, blood group, city, and health declaration.

Does Bloodwaale charge for blood?

Bloodwaale states that it does not charge for blood itself. Applicable blood bank processing fees and minimal platform or service fees may still apply.

Can Bloodwaale help with rare blood groups in India?

Bloodwaale positions itself as a network that supports urgent blood sourcing, including cases where rare blood groups need faster coordination across donors and blood banks.
